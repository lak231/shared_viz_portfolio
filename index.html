Our works

Antigua Sugar Mills

Antigua is an island with a very rich history; the sugar mills on the island are remnants of an era of slavery, and thus are an integral part of the island's history. Our client wishes to digitize the information of the sugar mills for access to scholars, visitors and lay people interested in the island. Our deliverable is an interactive map of Antigua and its sugar mills.

  • Client Griot Institute
  • Completed on December 2017
  • Skills HTML5 / Javascript

A day of a college student

Inspired by the beautiful visualization A Day in the Life of Americans by Nathan Yau from Flowing Data, we wish to create a similar visualization for college student like myself. Our main question for this project is how U.S. full-time college students spend their day. Specifically, We wanted to visualize the duration and the transition, examine the distribution and the differences of the activities by groups.

  • Client N/A
  • Completed on May 2018
  • Skills Javascript / D3
